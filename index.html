PRODUCTION
MANAGEMENT PROCESS

  • FREE QUOTE and CONTRACT AGREEMENT

    Preliminary parts analysis of your drawings and requirements versus chosen production process. Quote on tooling and parts cost according to quantities. Quote on drawing optimization to reduce tooling and/or part cost (if needed). Tooling longevity and ownership. Parts cost versus quantity and lead time.

  • TOOLING and JIGS FABRICATION

    Complete DFM and engineering for production with supplier. Manufacturing procedure for all tooling and jigs. Fabrication follow-up.

  • FIRST OFF, SAMPLES and T1

    Reception and quality control of "First Shot". Optimisation of tooling and/or jigs up to sample approval. Quality control and documentation follow-up.

  • PRE-PRODUCTION and PRODUCTION

    Pre-production of minimal quantity for testing purposes. Tooling and jig adjustments for final approval. Production of parts. Complete or Sub- assembly of parts as requested.

  • QUALITY CONTROL

    Reception of goods and quality control implementation. Inspected, tested, and validated as requested. Foursplines certification, and packaging.

  • DELIVERY TO YOUR FACILITY

    Manufacturing facility. Manufacturer contract for final assembly. Customers or distribution channels.

PRODUCTION
MANAGEMENT PROCESS

  • FREE QUOTE and CONTRACT AGREEMENT

    Preliminary parts analysis according to your drawings and requirements versus chosen production process. Quote on tooling and parts cost and according to requirements and (CAL) compliancy acceptance list. If needed, quote on drawing optimization to reduce tooling and/or part cost.

  • TOOLING and JIGS FABRICATION

    Complete DFM for production with suppliers. Manufacturing procedure for all tooling and jigs. Fabrication follow-up.

  • FIRST OFF, SAMPLES and T1

    Reception and quality control of "First off". Tooling optimization and/or jigs up to sample approval. Quality control and documentation update.

  • PRE-PRODUCTION and PRODUCTION

    Pre-production of minimal quantity for testing purposes. Tooling and jig adjustments for final approval. Production of parts. Complete or Sub-assembly of parts as requested.

  • QUALITY CONTROL

    Reception of goods and quality control implementation. Inspected, tested, and validated as requested. Foursplines certification and packaging.

  • DELIVERY TO YOUR FACILITY

    Manufacturing facility. Manufacturer contract for final assembly. Customers or distribution channels.

  • FOLLOW UP AND CUSTOMER SERVICE
